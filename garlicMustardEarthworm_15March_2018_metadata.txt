\f0\b\fs24 \cf0 garlicMustardEarthworm_15March_2018\

\b0 \
This spreadsheet includes all of the abundance data of all the species at the sites and treatments where the earthworms were sampled during the summer of 2017. Shannon diversity, total stem diversity, earthworm biomass, fern abundance, for abundance, grass abundance,  non native plant abundance, and woody plant abundance values are also included in this data sheet.\
\

\b Columns:	\

\b0 plot_ID: Unique ID for each plot for each year of data. Year-season-site-replicate#\
\

\b year:
\b0  Year data was collected. Continuous format of year. Years include 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017.\
\

\b site: 
\b0 Site where data was collected. Factor with 2 levels. See site abbreviation definitions and site treatments below\
\

\b Treatment:
\b0  All treatments. Factor with 3 levels (Invaded, Eradicated, Control).\
\

\b replicate: 
\b0 Replicate number for the treatment at the site. Each treatment replicated 3 times per site.\
 \
