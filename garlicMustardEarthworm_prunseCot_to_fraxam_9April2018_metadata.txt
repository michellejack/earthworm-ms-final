\b0 This spreadsheet includes all of the abundance data of all the species at the sites and treatments where the earthworms were sampled during the summer of 2017. Other species with abundances listed as \'930\'94 are from other sites in the full eradication experiment. This data was collated after we obtained new abundance data where values previously listed for 
\i Prunus 
\i0 cotyledons were ID\'92ed as 
\i Fraxinus americana 
\i0 seedlings (fraxam). Shannon diversity, total stem diversity, earthworm biomass, fern abundance, for abundance, grass abundance,  non native plant abundance, and woody plant abundance values are also included in this data sheet.\
\

\b Columns:	\
plotID: 
\b0 Unique ID for each plot for each year of data. Year-season-site-replicate#\
\

\b year:
\b0  Year data was collected. Year includes only 2017 data.\
\

\b site: 
\b0 Site where data was collected. Factor with 2 levels. See site abbreviation definitions and site treatments below\
\

\b treatment:
\b0  All treatments. Factor with 3 levels (Invaded, Eradicated, Control).\
\

\b replicate: 
\b0 Replicate number for the treatment at the site. Each treatment replicated 3 times per site.\
